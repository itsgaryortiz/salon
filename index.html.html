Frequently Asked Questions

What Hair Products Do You Recommend for Maintaining Balayage?

To maintain your balayage, use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners that nourish and protect color. Add a purple toner to neutralize brassiness, and a leave-in conditioner for hydration. Regular trims keep your style fresh and vibrant.

How Often Should I Get My Hair Colored?

You should refresh your hair color every 6 to 8 weeks for vibrant results. This keeps your color looking fresh and minimizes noticeable roots, ensuring you stay on-trend while maintaining healthy, beautiful hair.
